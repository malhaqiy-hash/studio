# **App Name**: OnTapp - Business Discovery Network

## Core Features:

- AI Intent Discovery: An AI tool that analyzes natural language queries to intelligently search and rank matching businesses, products, and services based on user intent.
- B2B Smart Matchmaker: An AI tool that evaluates business profile compatibility to suggest strategic partnerships with calculated match scores and reasoning.
- SyncAuth Role Management: Secure registration and login flows with specific access levels for Buyers, Sellers, Service Providers, and Enterprises.
- Business Identity Hub: Management of comprehensive business profiles including logo and cover image uploads using Firebase Storage.
- Hybrid Social Feed: A multi-type social stream allowing businesses to post products, services, opportunities, and industry knowledge.
- Global Pulse Messaging: Real-time messaging system for direct business-to-business communication and conversation tracking.
- Strategic Opportunity Stream: A dedicated feed for high-value business opportunities such as sourcing suppliers, distributors, or investment leads.
- Multilingual Bridge: Automatic translation layer supporting English, Indonesian, Japanese, Chinese, and Korean for global networking.
- Analytical Insight Dashboard: Centralized metrics display for tracking post engagement, follower growth, and messaging activity.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Palette: Deep Navy (#0F172A) for a foundation of authority and professional contrast.
- Action Accent: Electric Indigo (#6366F1) used for primary call-to-actions and interactive UI states.
- Headline and Body: 'Inter' sans-serif family for maximum legibility and professional clarity across data-heavy views.
- Minimalist, lightweight outline icons that maintain a clean, tech-native business aesthetic.
- Modular card-based grid system with a mobile-first responsive sidebar and spacious white space.
- Precise micro-interactions for button feedback and smooth slide-in transitions for side panels and notifications.
